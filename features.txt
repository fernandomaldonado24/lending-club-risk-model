Core loan features
loan_amnt: Loan requested.
funded_amnt: Amount approved.
funded_amnt_inv: Amount funded by investors.
term: Loan duration (36/60).
int_rate: Interest rate (risk proxy).
installment: Monthly payment.

Credit quality (top signal)
grade: Credit rating (A–G).
sub_grade: More granular rating.
fico_range_low / high: Credit score range.

Borrower profile
emp_length: Years employed.
home_ownership: Housing status.
annual_inc: Income.
verification_status: Income verified or not.
purpose: Loan purpose.


Debt & usage
dti: Debt-to-income ratio.
revol_bal: Revolving balance.
revol_util: Credit usage %.
total_acc: Total accounts.
open_acc: Open accounts.

Credit behavior
delinq_2yrs: Past delinquencies.
inq_last_6mths: Credit inquiries.
pub_rec: Public records.
pub_rec_bankruptcies: Bankruptcies.
tax_liens: Tax issues.

Credit history
earliest_cr_line: Oldest account.

Account status / structure
acc_now_delinq: Currently delinquent accounts.
collections_12_mths_ex_med: Collections last year.
chargeoff_within_12_mths: Recent charge-offs.
delinq_amnt: Amount delinquent.

Balances & limits
tot_cur_bal: Total balance.
total_rev_hi_lim: Revolving limit.
tot_hi_cred_lim: Total credit limit.
total_bal_ex_mort: Balance excl. mortgage.
total_bc_limit: Credit card limit.
total_il_high_credit_limit: Installment limit.

Utilization / efficiency (very strong)
bc_util: Card utilization.
bc_open_to_buy: Available credit.
avg_cur_bal: Avg balance per account.
pct_tl_nvr_dlq: % never delinquent.
percent_bc_gt_75: High utilization cards.

Activity & recency
acc_open_past_24mths: Recent accounts opened.
num_tl_op_past_12m: Accounts opened last year.
mths_since_recent_bc / inq: Recency of activity.
mo_sin_*: Months since events (aging metrics).

Account composition (counts)
Number of different account types and delinquency patterns:
num_bc_tl: Credit cards.
num_il_tl: Installment loans.
num_rev_accts: Revolving accounts.
num_tl_90g_dpd_24m: Serious delinquencies.

Mortgage
mort_acc: Mortgage accounts.

Meta / technical
initial_list_status: Listing type.
